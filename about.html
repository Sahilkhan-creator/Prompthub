About MintPrompt

A modern platform for high-quality AI prompt templates and tools

What is MintPrompt?

MintPrompt is a curated prompt hub designed to help creators, developers, writers, and AI enthusiasts generate better results using AI tools. The platform offers structured prompt templates, fixed prompts, and ready-to-use ideas across multiple categories.

Our Mission

Our mission is to simplify prompt creation and make advanced AI usage accessible to everyone. MintPrompt focuses on clarity, quality, and usability—ensuring that every prompt delivers consistent and effective results.

Ownership & Operations

MintPrompt is operated under Auric LLC and is owned by Sahil Khan. The platform is independently developed and maintained with a strong focus on performance, privacy, and user experience.

Developer

Sahil Khan is the developer behind MintPrompt. He is responsible for the design, development, and ongoing improvements of the platform, including frontend systems, prompt logic, and user experience.

Transparency & Trust

MintPrompt does not collect unnecessary personal data and follows privacy-first practices. All content is created to be informative, ethical, and compliant with advertising and platform policies.

Looking Ahead

MintPrompt continues to evolve with new prompt templates, premium features, and tools aimed at improving productivity and creativity in the AI ecosystem.
